## Action ideas
- Improve Transparency in Account Statements — Provide clear and detailed account statements, including all charges and transactions, to help customers understand their accounts better.
- Enhance Trading Experience — Address issues with contract prices, execution, and trading tools to prevent losses and improve overall user satisfaction.
- Streamline Customer Support — Train customer support agents to handle queries efficiently, reduce wait times, and ensure that issues are resolved promptly without requiring multiple follow-ups.
- Improve Order Execution System — Review and refine the order execution system to prevent incorrect execution of limit orders and ensure that stop losses are triggered accurately.
- Enhance Customer Support — Provide comprehensive training to customer support representatives to ensure they can effectively address customer queries and resolve issues in a timely manner.
- Resolve Technical Issues — Invest in infrastructure and resources to resolve technical issues such as app crashes, balance discrepancies, and payment failures, and ensure a seamless user experience.
- Conduct Thorough Testing Before Updates — Implement a rigorous testing process before releasing new updates to ensure that the app is stable and functions as expected.
- Improve Customer Support — Enhance customer support by providing timely and effective solutions to customer issues, rather than just apologizing for the inconvenience.
- Simplify and Optimize App Layout — Re-evaluate the app's layout and simplify it to improve the user experience and reduce clutter, making it easier for users to execute trades.
- Review and Simplify Fee Structure — Re-evaluate the current fee structure to ensure it is transparent, easy to understand, and competitive. Consider clearly displaying all charges before order confirmation.
- Improve Advertised Pricing Accuracy — Ensure that advertised pricing, such as '0% delivery charge', accurately reflects the final costs to customers, including any additional fees or taxes.
- Enhance Scalping Mode Features — Consider adding long short tools to the scalping mode to improve user experience and address customer feedback.
- Improve Broker Services — Evaluate and potentially upgrade broker services to better meet the needs of professional traders and improve overall satisfaction.
- Explore 3-in-1 Account Options — Research the feasibility of partnering with a bank to offer 3-in-1 accounts, which could enhance the app's appeal and convenience for users.
